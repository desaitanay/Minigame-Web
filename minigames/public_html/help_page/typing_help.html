How to play Typing-Speed

Overview

This is a standard typing speed game where you can get maximum of 5 minutes to show off your skills. Start typing and compete against your friends to see who's the fastest.

How to Play

  • Adjust the timer and click on start to start playing
  • Click on button to restart the game. Also, resets the paragrapgh.
  • The game will automatically compile your accuracy.
  • Game ends when the timer runs out.

Settings

  • Your highest score will be displayed on the bottom panel.
