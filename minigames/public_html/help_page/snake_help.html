How to play Snake

Overview

This is the snake game where you can control your snake.

How to Play

  • Start button to begin the game.
  • Reset button to play again, or reset.
  • Control snake direction with arrow keys.
  • Reaching target gives 1 point, and adds an extra segment to your snake.
  • Running into a wall or your own segments will end the game.

Settings

  • Settings icon on the left panel lets you select your snake / target color.
  • Your highest score will be displayed in the right panel.

Ouroboros Condition

You will be playing against an AI snake.

  • The rival snake will also attempt to get to the target, and get 1 segment larger if it reaches it.
  • The rival is especially hungry and will eat parts of itself to get to the target, if it redirects over itself it will lose segments.
  • If the rival crosses the player snake, it will lose a segment. If it reduces to two or less segments, it resets.
