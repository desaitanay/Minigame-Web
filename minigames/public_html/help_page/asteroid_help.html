How to play Asteroids

Overview

This is the Asteroids game where you control your ship and shoot at asteroids.

Rules

  • If you successfully shoot an asteroid, your score will increase by 1.
  • For every 10 asteroids that you successfully shoot, the level will increase, causing all current asteroids to be cleared, and all new asteroids to move at a faster speed.
  • If an asteroid makes contact with your ship, the game is over.

How to Play

  • Click the "Start game" to begin the game.
  • Rotate ship left by clicking W key or left arrow key
  • Rotate ship right by clicking D key or right arrow key
  • Move ship forward by clicking W key or forward up key
  • Shoot projectile by clicking spacebar
